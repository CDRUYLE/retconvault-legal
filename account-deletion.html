Delete Your RetconVault Account & Data

RetconVault (operated by RetConLabs). Last updated: July 12, 2026.

This page explains how to request deletion of your RetconVault account and the data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete your account inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open the RetconVault app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the prompt. Your account and all associated collection data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

support@retconvault.com

Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity and delete your account within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Your account (email address and hashed password).
  • Your entire collection: comics, games, grades, photos, valuations, notes, and any Pro entitlement records tied to your account.

What data is kept (and for how long)

  • Purchase/transaction records required for tax, accounting, or legal compliance may be retained for up to 7 years, as required by law. These records do not include your app collection data.
  • Subscription status is managed by Google Play; to stop billing, also cancel your subscription in the Google Play Store.

Delete some data without deleting your account

You can remove individual items from your collection at any time inside the app (open an item → Delete) without deleting your whole account.

Contact
